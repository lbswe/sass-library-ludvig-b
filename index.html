Zelda: Twilight princess

"The Legend of Zelda: Twilight Princess" is an action-adventure video game developed and published by Nintendo. It was released for the Nintendo GameCube and Wii consoles in 2006 and later re-released for the Wii U in 2016. Set in the fictional kingdom of Hyrule, the game follows the story of Link, the series' iconic protagonist. In "Twilight Princess," Hyrule is threatened by a dark force known as the Twilight Realm, which seeks to engulf the land in darkness. Link, a young farmhand from the village of Ordon, is chosen by fate to embark on a perilous journey to save Hyrule.

Zelda: Skyward Sword

"The Legend of Zelda: Skyward Sword" is an action-adventure video game developed and published by Nintendo. It was released in 2011 for the Wii console and marked the franchise's 25th anniversary. Skyward Sword serves as a prequel to the entire Zelda series, chronicling the origin of the Master Sword and the eternal conflict between good and evil. In "Skyward Sword," players assume the role of Link, a young knight-in-training who resides in the floating island city of Skyloft. The story revolves around the quest to rescue Link's childhood friend, Zelda, who has been taken by a malevolent force known as Demise. To save her and unravel the mysteries of their world, Link must embark on a journey through the vast sky and the perilous surface below.

Zelda: Breath Of The Wild

"The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild" is an open-world action-adventure video game developed and published by Nintendo. It was released in 2017 as a launch title for the Nintendo Switch console and also made available for the Wii U. Set in the expansive kingdom of Hyrule, the game revolutionized the Zelda franchise with its vast open-world exploration and nonlinear gameplay. In "Breath of the Wild," players assume the role of Link, who awakens from a century-long slumber to find Hyrule in a state of ruin. The once-great kingdom has fallen to the destructive power of Calamity Ganon, an ancient evil that threatens to consume Hyrule once again. With only fragmented memories of the past, Link embarks on a monumental quest to defeat Ganon and save Princess Zelda and the kingdom.
